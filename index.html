Haiyang Sun 孙海洋


I'm a PhD student in Dynamic Analysis Group at the Faculty of Informatics of Università della Svizzera italiana in Lugano, Switzerland.
My research interests include Program Analysis and Cloud computing. My supervisor is Prof. Walter Binder.

I received the BSc and MSc degrees (advised by Prof. Zhengwei Qi) in Software Engineering, from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (上海交通大学) in 2012 and 2015 respectively.

My email is haiyang.sun at usi dot ch.
